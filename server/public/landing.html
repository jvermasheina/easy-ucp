Frequently Asked Questions

Which platforms do you support?

All of them. WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, Shopify, PrestaShop, custom platforms — if you can export your products as a CSV or JSON file, we can make them AI-discoverable.

What is UCP?

Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) is the open standard launched by Google in January 2026 that enables AI agents like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini to discover, browse, and recommend products. Think of it as "SEO for AI shopping."

How does checkout work?

When an AI agent shows your product to a customer, the customer clicks through to your store's existing checkout page. We handle discovery — you handle the sale. No changes to your payment or fulfillment process.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. If you can export a CSV from your store (every platform supports this), you can upload it to Easy UCP. We also provide an API for developers who want to automate syncing.

Is my data secure?

We only store public product information (names, prices, descriptions, URLs). Never customer data, payment information, or sensitive business data. GDPR-compliant.

What if my platform adds native UCP later?

By then, you'll have been building AI visibility and referral data. Most platforms won't add native UCP support for 12-24 months. Early movers capture the advantage.
