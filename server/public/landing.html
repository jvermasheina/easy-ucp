All of them. We're platform-agnostic. WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, Shopify, custom platforms - if you can export products (CSV, JSON, API), we can make them AI-discoverable.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. If you can upload a spreadsheet, you can use Easy UCP Hub. No OAuth, no webhooks, no plugins.

Our analytics and AI traffic tracking will still be valuable. Plus, most platforms won't add native support for 18-24 months. You'll capture early-mover advantage.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We only store public product information. Never customer data, payment info, or sensitive business data.
