Frequently Asked Questions

What is UCP and why does my store need it?

Universal Commerce Protocol (UCP) is Google's new standard that makes your products discoverable to AI shopping agents like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. Without UCP integration, AI agents can't find or recommend your products—meaning you're invisible to the fastest-growing shopping channel. UCP integration ensures AI shoppers discover your store when they ask for product recommendations.

Which e-commerce platforms do you support?

Easy UCP Hub works with any e-commerce platform—WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, custom builds, and more. We're platform-agnostic by design. If you sell products online, we can integrate UCP for your store regardless of your platform choice.

How long does implementation actually take?

Your store becomes AI-discoverable within 2 weeks from signup. This includes our team setting up your UCP endpoint, configuring your product catalog, testing the integration, and going live. Compare this to 6+ months for custom development or indefinite waiting for platform-native UCP support.

What if my platform adds native UCP support later?

You keep your lifetime Easy UCP Hub access regardless. Even if WooCommerce or Magento eventually adds native UCP, you've already captured months or years of AI commerce sales. Plus, our multi-platform approach means you're never locked in—migrate platforms without losing UCP integration.
